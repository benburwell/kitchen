title: Sweet Potato Bean Burgers
yield: 6-8 patties
ingredients:
  - item: medium sweet potato
    qty: 1
    unit: 
  - item: white beans (cannellini)
    qty: 16
    unit: oz
    prep: drained and rinsed
  - item: white onion
    qty: 0.5
    unit: cup
    prep: chopped 
  - item: tahini
    qty: 2-3
    unit: T
  - item: apple cider vinegar
    qty: 0.75
    unit: t
  - item: garlic powder
    qty: 1
    unit: t
  - item: salt
    qty: 0.5
    unit: t
  - item: black pepper
    qty: 0.25
  - item: flour
    qty: 0.3
    unit: cup
  - item: coconut or canola oil
    qty: 1
    unit: T
  - item: panko breadcrumbs  
  - item: avocado
  - item: tomato
  - item: burger buns
procedure:
  - Bake sweet potato in 400º oven for 40-60 minutes or until tender.
  - Rinse beans with hot water. Add potato and beans to a large mixing bowl, and mash well. Fold in the onion and continue mashing. 
  - Add remaining ingredients to the bowl and mash until thickened. 
  - *Optional: Chill mixture in fridge for 30 minues to make forming patties easier.
  - Heat oven to 400º.
  - Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add coconut or canola oil. 
  - Form burger mixture into patties, coat in panko crumbs, and place in hot skillet. Cook 1-3 minutes on each side until brown, longer if mixture was chilled. 
  - Place the browned patties on a baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes, unitl cooked through.
  - Slice toppings and serve warm on toasted bun.

  - Store leftover burgers sealed in the fridge for 1 day or freeze and eat within a few weeks. 
  - To reheat, warm in 400º oven for 12 minutes or until warmed through.
