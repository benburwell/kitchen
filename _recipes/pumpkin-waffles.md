# vim: ft=yaml
title: Pumpkin Waffles
ingredients:
  - item: AP flour
    qty: 2.5
    unit: c
  - item: packed brown sugar
    qty: 0.3
    unit: c
  - item: baking powder
    qty: 2.25
    unit: t
  - item: baking soda
    qty: 0.5
    unit: t
  - item: kosher salt
    qty: 0.5
    unit: t
  - item: cinnamon
    qty: 2
    unit: t
  - item: ground ginger
    qty: 1
    unit: t
  - item: ground cloves
    qty: 0.25
    unit: t
  - item: eggs
    qty: 4
  - item: buttermilk
    qty: 1
    unit: c
  - item: butter
    qty: 6
    unit: t
  - item: canned pumpkin
    qty: 15
    unit: oz
  - item: canola spray
procedure:
  - sift together dry ingredients. Whisk eggs, add melted butter, buttermilk, and pumpkin until even.
  - Add wet to dry and stir until just combined.
  - Let the dough rest for 5 minutes.
  - Spray iron with canola oil and cook waffles. 
  - Transfer waffles to 250º oven to keep warm if desired.
